ABOUT US

Guided by the Centre for Psychological services and Career Development(PsyCad),Mente offers a broad spectrum on physchological services
to the staff and students of the University of Johannesburg.

According to the South African College of Applied Psychology(2018) suggests that one in six South Africans suffers from anxiety,
depression or a substance-use problems(and this does not include more serious conditions such as bipolar disorder or schizophrenia).
Dedicated to well-being and mental health of UJ's student and staff,with the partnership of PsyCad,we as Mente will help students
enagage with professional councellors and peer mentors.
